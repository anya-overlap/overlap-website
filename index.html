Overlap FAQs

There are hundreds of dating apps, yet few work reliably. We believe the market needs a universal dating app that delivers transparent results with a focus on compatiblity. We emphasize match curation vs. "numbers game" match discovery.
Unlike other apps, our “algorithm” isn't mysterious. Overlap only acts on data that users give themselves, so there's no need to teach it anything. We collect preferences and compatibility info and only show matches that fit.

For an even more personalized matching algorithm, answer questions in the Question Hub to find a match as unique as you!
Curious about AI usage in dating? There are various other apps on the market using AI.

AI can make good guesses about what you want, but we believe nothing beats asking you directly and using your exact preferences to guide a personalized search. We can envision smaller AI features in Overlap (e.g. suggesting first-meet spots based on mutual interests + location) but generally do not believe AI has a place at the core of the dating experience.

How did we get here?

In the 1990s, online dating featured detailed written profiles and often lengthy messages. Users spent longer crafting their profiles and messages, leading to lower volume of interactions.

In 2012, Tinder transformed online dating by letting users create instant profiles with just a few photos, no bio required. Its signature swipe feature added a game-like element to the experience.

At Overlap, we see value in both approaches. Our goal is a middle ground: some emphasis on profile creation, thoughtful messaging, and a focus on meaningful connections - still a modern dating experience but without the slot machine-style instant gratification.
A major issue with traditional dating apps is the incentives are not aligned: they only profit when people are actively dating. Is there a win-win solution?

We believe Overlap has found the middle ground. Casual daters using the free tier can connect without cost, while those on the Intentional tier gain an enhanced experience designed to help them find meaningful matches.

Our goal is to strike a balance: offer an upgraded experience for those who want it, without diminishing the experience for everyone else.
At Overlap, we recognize that meeting online is inherently inorganic - these are people outside your social circles, often with no mutual friends or shared backgrounds. While we can’t solve that directly, we only display matches seeking similar connections and highlight shared hobbies and activities to help you bond.

A two-pronged approach: we generally recommend meeting people in real life as well, since many nuances can't be captured in a dating profile. Long term, we intend to host in-person events that emphasize compatibility.
