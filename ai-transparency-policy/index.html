AI Transparency Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This AI Transparency Policy explains how Overlap uses artificial intelligence tools in connection with our business and product development.

We believe users should understand when AI is involved and, just as importantly, when it is not.

1. Overview

Overlap may use AI-assisted tools internally to help our team research, write, edit, debug, test, or otherwise accelerate parts of the software development process.

These tools may support our employees and contractors as they build and maintain the Overlap service, but they do not replace our responsibility for the final product.

2. How We Use AI Internally

Examples of internal AI-assisted development use may include:

  • Drafting or reviewing code
  • Helping identify bugs or implementation issues
  • Generating technical documentation or internal notes
  • Assisting with developer workflow, productivity, and experimentation

Any AI-assisted output used in development is reviewed, tested, and evaluated by humans before it is adopted into our software.

3. Where We Do Not Use AI

Overlap does not use AI to power its core matching or compatibility algorithms.

Specifically, AI is not used to:

  • Rank or score your matches
  • Make compatibility determinations between users
  • Personalize or generate your core algorithmic recommendations
  • Substitute for the logic behind our matching system

Our position is simple: AI may help us build software, but it is not used as part of the algorithm that determines user compatibility or match ordering.

4. Human Responsibility and Oversight

Our team remains responsible for product decisions, technical implementation, testing, and quality control.

If we use AI-assisted tools during development, those tools are used as support systems for our team. Final decisions about code, features, and system behavior are made by people.

5. Future Changes

If our use of AI materially changes, including if we ever decide to use AI in a way that affects the product experience, matching logic, or user-facing algorithmic decisions, we may update this policy and revise the effective date above.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this AI Transparency Policy, contact us at contact@tryoverlap.app.
